# OJ Pitch Narrative — The Story in 2 Minutes

OJ is a fresh, human, mobile-first approach to Workforce Management.  
It’s built around a simple idea:

> Workforce Management shouldn’t feel overwhelming.  
> It should feel like a fresh start.

Every morning, managers wake up to a long list of responsibilities:
- shifts to fill  
- absences to approve  
- compliance issues to resolve  
- timesheets to check  
- people to support  

Most WFM tools respond with dashboards, menus, and complexity.

OJ takes a different approach.

---

# 🍊 The Big Idea

OJ is inspired by something universal:  
**the feeling of starting your day with a glass of orange juice.**

It’s energising.  
It’s simple.  
It sets the tone for the day.

OJ brings that same clarity to Workforce Management.

---

# 🍊 The OJ Wheel

A standard orange has **10 natural segments**.  
OJ uses this structure to represent the 10 core areas of WFM:

1. Shifts  
2. Time & Attendance  
3. Absence  
4. Forecasting  
5. Planning  
6. Succession  
7. Skills  
8. Compliance  
9. Employee Experience  
10. Insights  

Each segment contains tasks or alerts.  
As tasks are completed, the segment “empties” — like drinking your morning juice.

When all segments are empty, the user has “finished their OJ” for the day.

This creates a simple, intuitive, action-first workflow.
