# OJ Narrative — The Story Behind the Product

OJ is built around a simple idea:  
**Workforce Management shouldn’t feel overwhelming. It should feel like a fresh start.**

Every morning, managers and employees in shift-based environments wake up to a long list of responsibilities:
- shifts to fill  
- absences to approve  
- compliance issues to resolve  
- timesheets to check  
- forecasts to review  
- people to support  

Most WFM tools respond to this complexity with even more complexity — dashboards, menus, reports, and alerts scattered across multiple systems.

OJ takes a different approach.

---

# 🍊 A Fresh Start for Workforce Management

OJ is inspired by something universal:  
**the feeling of starting your day with a glass of orange juice.**

It’s energising.  
It’s simple.  
It sets the tone for the day.

OJ brings that same clarity and freshness to Workforce Management.

Instead of overwhelming users with everything they *could* do, OJ shows them only what they *need* to do — today, right now, in the moment.

---

# 🍊 The OJ Wheel — A New Mental Model

A standard orange has **10 natural segments**, and OJ uses this structure to represent the 10 core areas of Workforce Management:

1. Shift Management  
2. Time & Attendance  
3. Absence Management  
4. Forecasting  
5. Workforce Planning  
6. Succession Planning  
7. Skills & Competency  
8. Compliance  
9. Employee Experience  
10. Insights & Analytics  

Each segment contains tasks or alerts.  
As tasks are completed, the segment “empties” — like drinking your morning juice.

When all segments are empty, the user has “finished their OJ” for the day.

This creates a simple, intuitive workflow that keeps the workforce moving.

# 🍊 A Companion to the HCM, Not a Replacement

OJ doesn’t try to replace the core HR or WFM system.  
Instead, it acts as a **lightweight companion layer** that surfaces the actions that matter most.

The HCM remains the source of truth.  
OJ becomes the source of clarity.

This makes OJ easy to integrate, easy to adopt, and easy to love.
