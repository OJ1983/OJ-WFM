# OJ Capability Map — The 10 Segments of Workforce Management

OJ uses the natural structure of an orange — typically **10 segments** — to represent the 10 core areas of Workforce Management (WFM).  
Each segment contains tasks, alerts, or insights that help managers start their day with clarity.

This capability map shows how each segment aligns to real WFM needs and how OJ simplifies them into an action-first experience.

---

## 🍊 1. Shift Management
**What it covers:**
- Creating and editing shifts  
- Publishing schedules  
- Managing shift swaps  
- Handling last-minute changes  

**Why it matters:**  
Shift management is the heartbeat of WFM. OJ surfaces only the actions that need attention today — no clutter.

---

## 🍊 2. Time & Attendance
**What it covers:**
- Clock-ins and clock-outs  
- Timesheet approvals  
- Exception handling (late, early, missed punches)  

**Why it matters:**  
Accurate time capture reduces payroll errors and compliance risk. OJ highlights only the exceptions that need action.

---

## 🍊 3. Absence Management
**What it covers:**
- Holiday requests  
- Sick leave  
- Entitlement balances  
- Approvals and conflicts  

**Why it matters:**  
Absence is one of the biggest disruptors in shift-based work. OJ helps managers respond quickly and keep coverage stable.

---

## 🍊 4. Forecasting
**What it covers:**
- Labour demand  
- Volume predictions (sales, footfall, patient flow)  
- Seasonal patterns  

**Why it matters:**  
Forecasting helps managers plan ahead. OJ turns complex data into simple “what you need to know today” insights.

---

# 🍊 How the Segments Work Together

Each segment represents a category of daily actions.  
OJ’s home screen shows the **OJ Wheel** — a 10-segment orange.

- Segments with tasks appear “full”  
- Completing tasks “empties” the segment  
- When all segments are empty, the user has “finished their OJ” for the day  

This creates a simple, intuitive workflow that keeps the workforce moving.
