# OJ Brand Concept — Fresh Start Workforce

OJ is built around a simple, powerful metaphor:

> Just like a morning glass of orange juice gives you energy to start the day,  
> OJ gives managers and employees the clarity they need to take action.

The brand is intentionally:
- fresh  
- energetic  
- optimistic  
- mobile-first  
- human and friendly  

OJ’s visual identity is based on the natural structure of an orange, which typically has **10 segments**.  
These segments map to the 10 core areas of Workforce Management:

1. Shift Management  
2. Time & Attendance  
3. Absence Management  
4. Forecasting  
5. Workforce Planning  
6. Succession Planning  
7. Skills & Competency Tracking  
8. Compliance  
9. Employee Experience  
10. Insights & Analytics  

Each segment represents a category of tasks or alerts.  
As tasks are completed, the segment “empties” — like drinking your morning juice.

This creates a simple, intuitive daily workflow:
- Open OJ  
- See your segments  
- Complete your actions  
- Finish your OJ for the day  

OJ is designed to complement **ERP and core people platforms** by providing a lightweight, action-oriented layer — integrated on standard domains such as People, Positions, organisation, Approvers, and Projects.
