# OJ Personas — Understanding Our Users

OJ is designed for real people working in fast-moving, shift-based environments.  
These personas represent the primary users of OJ and help guide design, UX, and product decisions.

---

# 🍊 Persona 1 — The Frontline Manager  
**Name:** Sarah Thompson  
**Role:** Store Manager, Retail  
**Age:** 34  
**Tech Comfort:** Medium  
**Device:** Mobile-first (iPhone)  

### **Goals**
- Start each day knowing what needs attention  
- Keep the store fully staffed  
- Approve requests quickly  
- Stay compliant without reading long reports  
- Support her team  

### **Frustrations**
- Too many systems to check  
- Last-minute absences  
- Compliance rules that are hard to interpret  
- Forecasting buried in dashboards  
- Feeling reactive instead of proactive  

### **How OJ Helps**
- Shows only the tasks that matter today  
- Highlights urgent issues (e.g., uncovered shifts)  
- Provides simple compliance alerts  
- Gives her a “fresh start” every morning
