🍊 OJ Brand Mark — The Missing Segment Concept
Overview
The OJ brand mark is built around a simple but powerful idea:
the workforce is a puzzle, and people are the missing piece.

OJ’s visual identity centres on a 10‑segment circular form — inspired by an orange slice, a jigsaw, and the 10 core areas of Workforce Management. But unlike a perfect circle, the OJ mark intentionally features one missing segment.

This missing segment is not an absence — it is a statement.

🍊 The Missing Segment
Symbolism
The missing segment represents:

the unpredictability of daily operations

the gaps managers must fill

the human element at the centre of workforce management

the “jumble” of shift work, compliance, and scheduling

the operational challenges OJ helps solve

Workforce Management is rarely complete.
There is always a shift to fill, a person to support, a gap to close.

The missing segment makes this visible.

🍊 The Human Element
Inside the missing segment sits a minimal human icon — a soft, abstract figure that completes the circle.

This communicates:

people are the heart of operations

OJ is built around humans, not systems

the workforce is the missing piece managers assemble daily

OJ brings clarity to the human puzzle

The human icon is intentionally simple so it scales beautifully from app icon to billboard.

🍊 The Wheel
The remaining nine segments form a clean, geometric wheel:

warm citrus gradient

rounded edges for a friendly, human feel

evenly spaced to represent balance and clarity

symbolic of the 10 WFM capabilities

a nod to the orange metaphor (fresh start)

a nod to the jigsaw metaphor (Org Jigsaw)

The wheel is the system.
The missing segment is the problem.
The human icon is the solution.

🍊 Brand Meaning Layers
The mark supports every layer of the OJ brand:

OJ = Orange Juice
The wheel resembles an orange slice — a fresh start to the day.

OJ = Org Jigsaw
The missing segment becomes a literal puzzle piece.

OJ = Operational Jumpstart
The human icon is the spark that completes the system.

OJ = Owen Jones
A subtle personal signature embedded in the brand.

This layered meaning gives OJ a depth and richness uncommon in workforce products.

🍊 App Icon Usage
For the app icon:

the full wheel is used

the missing segment + human icon remain visible

background uses a warm citrus gradient

corners are rounded for iOS/Android harmony

slight inner shadow adds depth and tactility

The result is an icon that stands out instantly on a home screen.

🍊 Monochrome Version
In single‑colour applications:

the wheel becomes a bold outline

the missing segment becomes clean negative space

the human icon becomes a simple silhouette

The mark remains recognisable even at very small sizes.

🍊 Animation Concept
The missing segment mark is designed for motion:

The wheel appears complete.

One segment pops out and falls away.

The gap flickers through “negative” meanings:

Org Jumble

Operational Jam

Organisational Junk

Operating Jargon

The human icon fades in to fill the gap.

The full OJ mark locks into place.

Tagline appears:
“Your daily operational jumpstart.”

This animation becomes the signature OJ intro.

🍊 Tagline Pairings
The mark pairs best with:

Your daily operational jumpstart.

The missing piece of your workforce.

Solving the workforce puzzle, daily.

Where your workforce comes together.

Each reinforces the symbolism of the missing segment.

🍊 Summary
The OJ brand mark is more than a logo — it is a visual metaphor for the entire product philosophy.

It represents:

the daily workforce puzzle

the human element at the centre

the gaps managers must fill

the clarity OJ provides

the fresh start each day brings

It is simple, iconic, meaningful, and deeply tied to the product’s purpose.
